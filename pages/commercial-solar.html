$9,500
Size of the Panels:
40 x 200
Owner:

Contract Type:
Commercial Solar Panels
Manager:
Oleg Yudashkin
Location:
Albuquerque, MI

While this project seemed to be an easy one at a first glance, still all the area that the church and all its auxiliary buildings were taking up made us think a few times.
Eventually, choosing on which solar panels system configuration to offer to best cover the needs of the church, we’ve then moved to budgetary decisions.

Considering the type of this institution, we were happy to offer them pay in installments for our service and the panel.

Eventually, after we’ve checked all the size parameters and the regular power consumption, we were able to determine the right system.

Installing it took us just under 2 hours!
